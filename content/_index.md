## Research interests

My current research interests:

- various aspects of mathematical general relativity, including:
  - turbulent dynamics of asymptotically AdS spaces and confined gravity models
  - critical phenomena in gravitational collapse
  - singularities
  - cosmological solutions
- Hamiltonian partial differential equations on bounded domains
- numerical relativity
- numerical analysis of partial differential equations

More specifically, I use numerical methods and analytical techniques
to get insight into the structure of solutions to the nonlinear
evolution equations (like the Einstein equation).
